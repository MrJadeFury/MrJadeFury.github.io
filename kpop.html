The Beginning Stages

The first k-pop songs that hooked me into the kpop scene were all basically Twice songs. It all started with Likey. In the beginning, I thought of the song to be fairly catchy and worth my attention. Something stuck to me about it. The catchy vibe could have been it, but it also could have been the dance itself. I would always catch myself doing the choreography from time to time due to how entertaining it was.

The Next Stages

Eventually, after around two months, I began to listen to most of Twice's songs: Dance The Night Away, their newest release at the time, What Is Love, and of course, Likey. Some of their older songs also began to appear in my regular playlists such as TT, Cheer Up, and Like Ooh Ahh. I was slowly beginning to fall into a hole that I know I would never regret falling into.
